Western Massachusetts Amateur Radio Society

Greetings,

This radio 'club' is aimed towards:

  • POTA (Parks on the Air)
  • Emergency Preparedness
  • Field Day and Winter Field Day
  • Teaching the art of ham radio
  • Getting people licensed
  • And then getting them on the air!

This is a NO dues, NO assets club. We will not charge dues, and the club will not have a storehouse of gear.

This does not mean members won't help new members. And we may have a repeater at some point - But it will remain the property of the person or people who donate their time and resources to the larger radio community.

We hope to have periodic meet-ups for POTA or other events.

Membership will be open to anyone pending review of the existing membership committee.

If you have questions please email membershipwmaars.com
